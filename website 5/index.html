MidNightDinner

The background story of this series is in an Izakaya which only has 12 seats named Meshiya which is located in Shinjuku, Tokyo. Despite the shop's unusual opening hours of 12pm to 7am, this restaurant is popular in Shinjuku's busy nightlife. Set in Shinjuku, and its hours of operation, most of the stories in this series take place at night.
